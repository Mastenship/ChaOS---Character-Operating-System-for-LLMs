[KEEP: Purpose & Role] This template defines a character's interpersonal strategy and malevolent personality traits using the Dark Triad model. It assesses tendencies towards social manipulation, self-absorption, and a lack of empathy.

=== SECTION: TRAIT_DEFINITIONS ===

[Trait Definitions]
Narcissism: Grandiose self-view, entitlement, dominance, and a need for admiration.

Machiavellianism: Strategic manipulation, cynicism, a pragmatic and amoral approach to social interaction.

Psychopathy: High impulsivity, thrill-seeking, callousness, and a lack of remorse or empathy.
