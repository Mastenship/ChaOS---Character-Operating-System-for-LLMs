=== SECTION: TEMPORAL_FLOW_PROCESSING ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
This COT processes implicit passage of time through narrative, managing circadian phase, fatigue accumulation, routine pressures, and subjective time perception using chronobiology and temporal cognition. It translates time cues into:
- TEMPORAL_DYNAMICS tracking updates (group-scoped)
- TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS overlays for fatigue and routine pressure
- time-driven behavioral impulses for DECISION_SYNTHESIS
- a trigger signal for TEMPORAL_DECAY_RESOLUTION on major time skips

[KEEP: Relationship to Other Modules]
Informs: TEMPORAL_DYNAMICS, BODY (recovery only when explicit), GOALS_MOTIVATIONS (routine pull via modifiers), SKILLS (fatigue execution overlays), DECISION_SYNTHESIS (time-driven impulses).
Is Informed By: TEMPORAL_DYNAMICS baselines, narrative cues, TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS (existing overlays), MEMORY (temporal landmarks).

=== SECTION: NARRATIVE_TIME_EXTRACTION ===

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: Infer temporal progression from narrative without requiring explicit timestamps.

IMPLICIT_TIME_MARKERS:
- MEALS: BREAKFAST, LUNCH, DINNER, SNACK
- LIGHT: SUNRISE, SUNSET, DARKNESS, MIDNIGHT
- ROUTINES: MORNING_JOG, NIGHTCAP, CAMP_SETUP, BREAKING_CAMP

TRANSITION_MAGNITUDE:
- MICRO: minutes pass
- MINOR: less than 1 hour
- MODERATE: hours pass
- MAJOR: overnight or day transition
- MACRO: multiple days or more

- INFERRED_TIME_PASSAGE: [MOMENTS_LATER | AFTER_A_WHILE | NEXT_MORNING | DAYS_LATER | UNKNOWN]
- TRANSITION_MAGNITUDE: [MICRO | MINOR | MODERATE | MAJOR | MACRO]
