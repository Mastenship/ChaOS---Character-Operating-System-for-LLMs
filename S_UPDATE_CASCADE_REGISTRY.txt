=== SECTION: UPDATE_CASCADE_REGISTRY ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
This registry defines which COTs execute, in what order, and what they are responsible for proposing into:
- [COGNITIVE_SCRATCHPAD][PROPOSED_UPDATES_REGISTRY]
It is the system-wide API spec for canonical bracket-path proposal targets.

[KEEP: Relationship to Other Modules]
Informs: All COT templates and STATE_GOVERNANCE_AND_COMMIT tie-break behavior.
Is Informed By: STATE_GOVERNANCE_OVERLAY and CANONICAL_TOKEN_DICTIONARY.

=== SECTION: SYSTEM_WIDE_WRITE_POLICY ===

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: All COTs must follow this policy.

Policy:
- COTs do not write directly into persistent modules.
- COTs write intended changes as proposals into [COGNITIVE_SCRATCHPAD][PROPOSED_UPDATES_REGISTRY].
- STATE_GOVERNANCE_AND_COMMIT is the only writer to persistent templates and TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS.

Canonical_Path_Convention:
- Bracket-path tokens are machine-stable UPPER_SNAKE per S_CANONICAL_TOKEN_DICTIONARY.
- TARGET_FIELD values must be canonical bracket paths.
