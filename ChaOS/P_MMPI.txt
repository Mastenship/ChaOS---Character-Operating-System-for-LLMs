=== SECTION: CLINICAL_SCALE_MARKERS ===

Define each scale using the specified format: Scale Name (Scale #: [T-score/intuitive description]). 50th percentile (T-score of 50) represents the normal baseline. Do not add any additional interpretation or flavor text in this section.

=== SECTION: SCALE_DEFINITIONS ===

[Scale Definitions]
Scale 1 - Hypochondriasis: Excessive concern for personal health.

Scale 2 - Depression: Poor morale, lack of hope, and general dissatisfaction.

Scale 3 - Hysteria: Emotional instability and a tendency to develop physical symptoms in response to stress.

Scale 4 - Psychopathic Deviate: Difficulty with social norms and authority, potential for antisocial behavior.

Scale 6 - Paranoia: Suspiciousness, delusional thinking, and hypersensitivity.

Scale 7 - Psychasthenia: Excessive doubts, compulsions, obsessions, and anxiety.

Scale 8 - Schizophrenia: Social alienation, bizarre thoughts or behaviors, and potential for psychotic episodes.

Scale 9 - Hypomania: Elevated mood, excitability, and accelerated psychomotor activity.

Scale 10 - Social Introversion: Introversion, shyness, and withdrawal from social contact.
