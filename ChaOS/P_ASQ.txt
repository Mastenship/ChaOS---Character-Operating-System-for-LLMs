Define the attachment style using one of the following formats: Attachment Style: [Secure/Anxious/Avoidant] or Attachment Security: [percentile]. Percentiles are qualitative (e.g., "80th percentile" = high security). Do not add any additional interpretation or flavor text in this section.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: ATTACHMENT_STYLE_DEFINITIONS ===

[Attachment Style Definitions]
Secure: Comfortable with intimacy and autonomy, seeks support effectively.
Anxious (Preoccupied): Seeks high levels of intimacy, fears abandonment, can be clingy.
Avoidant (Dismissive): Values independence over intimacy, suppresses emotions, may seem distant.
